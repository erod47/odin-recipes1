15-Minute Tomato Gnocchi with Burrata

I am a huge foodie, but most of the time, I can't be bothered to cook

I found this recipe on Instagram from a private chef; it's incredibly simple with seven ingredients that you can buy anywhere, and this dish will have you feeling like you're living la dolce vita

Ingredients

  • 400g of gnocchi (I use a store-bought gnocchi)
  • 1 pint of cherry tomatos
  • A handful of diced onion
  • A tablespoon of tomato paste
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • One ball of burrata
  • Handful of basil for garnish
  • Optional: chili flakes on top for a kick
    1. Set a pot of water to a boil, and boil the gnocchi for 2 minutes
    2. While boiling, halve cherry tomatoes
    3. Take gnocchi off the boil, fry in a pan coated in olive oil for 5 minutes or until golden brown
    4. In a separate pan, sautee onions, tomato paste, and garlic
    5. Add sliced cherry tomatoes in pan, reduce for about 8 minutes or until it turns into a sauce
    6. Toss gnocchi in tomato sauce
    7. Move into a bowl, place a ball of burrata and basil on top
