Za'taar Eggs with Labneh on Toast

My most important meal of the day is breakfast; a good, healthy breakfast will immediately determine my path for the day. If the first thing in my mouth is fresh and delicious, it'll put me in a great mood, ready to take on the day.

I also found this recipe on Instagram. My favorite food is Middle Eastern/Mediterranean, so I knew this easy, 15-minute recipe would become a staple in my kitchen!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of sourdough bread
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 spoonfuls of Labneh
  • 2 tablespoons of zataar
  • 1 tablespoon of chili flakes
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil for frying
    1. Put slices of sourdough bread in the toaster
    2. Pour 1/4 cup of olive oil into a pan until it begins to bubble
    3. Take toast our of toaster, let cool
    4. Add zaatar and chili in olive oil, mix with spoon to create the sauce
    5. Crack eggs in pan to fry, use spoon to consistently pour the oil on the eggs
    6. While eggs fry, spread labneh on cooled toast,
    7. When satisfied, take eggs out and place them on top of the labneh toast
    8. Take spoonful of oil and drizzle on toast for extra flavor
